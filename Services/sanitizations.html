Air Duct Sanitization

The Duct System, used in air heating and cooling your home, is a collection of tubes/ducts that distributes the heated or cooled air to the various rooms. We all know that it is important to keep these ducts clean in order to keep the indoor air clean and healthy.

The Duct Sanitization is very important for you..

Sanitization prevent the spread of germs and spores through the air. Mold is the primary concern with ducts, as it can easily build up in these isolated areas and can be tough to remove. We use Benefect Botanical Disinfectant which is a herbal extract and 100% natural product.
